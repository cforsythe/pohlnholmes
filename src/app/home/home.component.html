The Basics

What we do

Pohl and Holmes, Incorporated has been providing California almond growers with quality hulling and shelling services since 1990. We take pride in putting our customers first, working hard to earn the trust that must be nurtured with each and every grower.

Harvesting

Pohl and Holmes, Inc. can assist you to coordinate your harvesting operations efficiently, even during the peak of harvest. A successful harvest begins with good harvesting techniques and product handling.

Drying

The drying services provided at Pohl & Holmes, Inc. provide on-site convienience for our customers. We can dry bulk loads or bins.

Sustainability

Pohl & Holmes, Inc. is dedicated to sustainable farming operations utilizing production practices that respect the environment, neighbors and employees. The result is a plentiful, nutritious and safe food product today and for future generations.
